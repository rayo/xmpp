XSF People :: Peter Saint-Andre

Peter Saint-Andre is a technical leader at Cisco and has served as executive director of the XMPP Standards Foundation since 2002. He has been contributing to the Jabber/XMPP developer community since late 1999, where he has focused on technology standardization as author of both the XMPP RFCs and numerous XMPP extension protocols.

Peter's weblog is published at stpeter.im and syndicated at Planet Jabber. His contact information is available here.
